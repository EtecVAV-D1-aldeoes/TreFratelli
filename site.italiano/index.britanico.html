Our history

A Tre Fratellihas its roots in 1925, when Giovanni decided to cultivate Chiavennasca — Montanha Nebbiolo — on the terraces overlooking Tirano. Amid economic crises, wars and the resurgence of Alpine tourism, the family preserved a simple tradition: wine, hospitality, stonework and patience..

During the difficult years, the barrels were protected in a small "secret cellar" carved into the rock; in the years of recovery, the grandchildren combined their agricultural knowledge with their studies in winemaking, never abandoning the dry-stone walls that support the mountain.

Today, the fourth generation continues the house with the same idea: good wine with good food, in a place that smells of wood, bread, and valley.

The Cellar

Small, essential, carved into the rock. Controlled fermentation, ageing in oak barrels and long maturation: the mountains demand time, and we respect it.

Mountain and Tradition

Artisanal wines inspired by the character of Valtellina: local grapes, hand harvesting and slow maturation. Each vintage reflects the climate and the work carried out on the terraces.
